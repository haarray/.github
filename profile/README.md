## Haarray

Haarray is a product organization built around disciplined, useful, long-term software.

The name is inspired by Hari, the founder's grandfather. The company name is **Haarray**.

### Product direction

Haarray is being reset around a cleaner modern web product direction, with **React** and **Tailwind** planned for the next serious build.

The strongest current product idea is **Harilog**, a personal finance and life logging system.

> Log money. Log life.

Harilog will help people record:

- expenses and income
- accounts and wallets
- categories and tags
- journals and reflections
- receipts and attachments
- portfolio and assets
- monthly summaries and reports

The goal is not to ship a demo. The goal is to build one strong product foundation first, then expand the Haarray product family.

### Future products

- **HariCMS** - future multi-project CMS / website engine.
- More Haarray/Hari family products after the first strong product foundation is clear.

### Product principles

- Build real products, not demos.
- Keep code clean, maintainable, and easy for one developer to reason about.
- Prefer small working vertical slices.
- Avoid over-engineering.
- Make software that remembers useful things.
